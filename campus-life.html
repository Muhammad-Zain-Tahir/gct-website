Beyond the Classroom

At Government College of Technology, Bhakkar, we believe in creating an environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and lifelong memories.

Student Societies & Clubs

Tech & Innovation Club

Participate in hackathons, robotics competitions, and workshops on AI, IoT, and Web Development.

Literary & Debating Society

Hone your public speaking, creative writing, and critical thinking skills through various events.

Sports & Adventure Club

From cricket to table tennis, stay active and represent GCT in inter-collegiate tournaments.

Cultural & Arts Society

Celebrate creativity through music, drama, fine arts, and cultural festivals.

Campus Facilities

Central Library

A treasure trove of knowledge with vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources.

Advanced Laboratories

State-of-the-art labs for each engineering department with modern equipment.

Hostel Accommodation

Safe, secure, and affordable on-campus hostel facilities fostering strong community.

Annual Events & Traditions

Fresher's Welcome

A grand event to welcome new students into the GCT Bhakkar family with fun and performances.

Technical Symposium

"INNOVATE GCT" - Flagship event with project exhibitions and research competitions.

Sports Gala

Exciting inter-departmental sports competition filled with energy and camaraderie.

Cultural Week

Multi-day extravaganza of music, drama, dance, and food stalls showcasing student talent.
